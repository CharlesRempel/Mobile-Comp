About the Companies

Bell Canada is a Canadian telecommunications and media company headquartered in Montreal, Quebec, providing a comprehensive and innovative suite of broadband communications and content services to consumer, residential business and government customers in Canada.
Click Here to Learn More About Bell Canada.

Telus is a Canadian national telecommunications company that provides a wide range of telecommunications products and services including internet access, voice, entertainment, healthcare, video, satellite and IPTV television.
Click Here to Learn More About Telus.

Koodo Mobile is a Canadian mobile brand started by Telus in 2008 and mostly oriented toward younger customers. Koodo differs from its parent Telus by not requiring a fixed term contract. Koodo currently provides postpaid and prepaid services.
Click Here to Learn More About Koodo.

Rogers Communications is a diversified public Canadian communications and media company. It operates particularly in the field of wireless communications, cable television, telephone, and Internet connectivity with significant additional telecommunications and mass media assets. The company is headquartered at 333 Bloor Street East in Toronto, Ontario.
